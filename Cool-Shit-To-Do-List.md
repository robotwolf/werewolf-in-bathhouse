# Cool Shit To Do List

Update rule:
- add ideas here when they become concrete enough to remember
- mark things as `Now`, `Soon`, `Later`, or `Moon-Later`
- keep the items short and sharp so this stays useful instead of becoming archaeology

## Moon-Later
- Build a sky treatment for the entry facade that can feature a meaningful overhead star arrangement.
- Use Lupus as the starting readable constellation over the bathhouse threshold.
- As moonrise advances, gradually distort the stars away from literal constellations into wolf-coded symbolic shapes.
- At full moon, experiment with subtle sky animation:
  - a wolf running
  - a deer fleeing
  - a human figure running
- Keep the sky event optional and reward player attention instead of screaming for it.

## Notes
