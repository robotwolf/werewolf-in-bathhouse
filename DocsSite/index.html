The Handoff Chain

Ginny

Chooses what rooms exist, how they connect, how long approaches get, and which special rooms are allowed to haunt the layout.

Mason

Builds stock or parametric shells when a room wants them. For hero authored rooms, he can remain attached and politely do nothing.

Stagehand

Publishes room truth: connectors, markers, labels, debug helpers, and the seam gameplay uses instead of geometry archaeology.

Gideon

Consumes the generated world at runtime: arrival, admission, POIs, crowd behavior, and other management-adjacent crimes.
